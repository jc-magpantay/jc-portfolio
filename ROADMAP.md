# Guiding Principles

## ✅ Show real work

Only include projects and experience that I have personally worked on.

No fake testimonials.

No placeholder content.

---

## ✅ Quality over quantity

A few excellent case studies are more valuable than many generic portfolio items.

---

## ✅ One Commit = One Feature

Every improvement should become its own Git commit.

Example:

* Improve Hero Section
* Rewrite About Section
* Create Featured Projects
* Add VFA Recruitment Funnel Case Study

---

## ✅ Think like a product

This portfolio is a long-term professional asset that will continue to grow throughout my career.
