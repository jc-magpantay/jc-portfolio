Technical Operations & CRM Management for an International EdTech Company

17-month engagement supporting Algorithmics and Clubhouse Engineers with CRM, LMS, documentation, content publishing, and technical operations.

Technical Operations Algorithmics / Clubhouse Engineers Aug 2024 – Jan 2026

Project Overview

Algorithmics is an international programming and STEM education company that teaches children aged 5 to 18 through project-based learning. The company operates globally through a franchise model supported by a proprietary learning platform, standardized curriculum, and cloud-based systems.

I supported Algorithmics and Clubhouse Engineers across technical operations, CRM management, LMS administration, documentation, website content management, and process improvement over a 17-month engagement.

Background & Business Problem

The business required someone who could manage multiple technical systems while keeping day-to-day operations organized and efficient. Responsibilities expanded beyond administrative support to include CRM data management, documentation, LMS administration, and content publishing.

As the organization scaled, these interconnected responsibilities required a technical and operational approach to ensure systems remained synchronized and workflows efficient.

My Role

I worked as a Technical Virtual Assistant responsible for multiple operational and technical tasks across the organization.

  • Managing CRM databases in Coda
  • Maintaining operational data structures
  • Creating and updating forms
  • Supporting CRM workflows and small automations
  • Managing sales pipelines and lead tracking
  • LMS administration, student and teacher management
  • Documentation and SOP creation
  • Website blog publishing and GitHub content management
  • AWS Amplify content deployment and technical support

Challenges & Obstacles

The early months required learning multiple new systems (notably Coda and GitHub workflows), understanding business processes, and managing interconnected operational databases while maintaining daily operations.

Challenge: Rapid onboarding across diverse technical systems and aligning them to business workflows.

Solution: Prioritized understanding each workflow, built small automations, and documented processes to reduce friction and enable smoother handoffs.

Solution & Approach

I focused on building a solid understanding of business processes before making changes, gradually implementing maintenance tasks, automations, documentation, and publishing workflows.

  1. Learned platform-specific workflows and data models.
  2. Organized operational data and CRM structures.
  3. Implemented small automations and workflow improvements.
  4. Standardized documentation and publishing processes.
  5. Deployed content through GitHub and AWS Amplify.

Results & Outcome

Over 17 months I managed CRM and operational databases, supported LMS operations, published content, and delivered operational improvements that supported an international remote team.
