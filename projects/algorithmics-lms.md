# Project Overview

Algorithmics is an international programming and STEM education company that teaches children aged 5 to 18 through project-based learning. The company operates globally through a franchise model supported by a proprietary learning platform, standardized curriculum, and cloud-based systems.

Alongside supporting Algorithmics operations, I also worked with Clubhouse Engineers, a technology education initiative managed by the same client. My responsibilities extended beyond traditional virtual assistance into technical operations, CRM management, LMS administration, documentation, website content management, and process improvement.

Over 17 months, I became responsible for maintaining several business systems that supported students, teachers, internal operations, marketing, and content publishing.

---

# The Business Problem

The business required someone who could manage multiple technical systems while keeping day-to-day operations organized and efficient.

The work involved much more than administrative support. It required maintaining CRM data, organizing documentation, publishing website content, supporting the learning management system, and integrating different tools used across the organization.

As the business expanded, these responsibilities became increasingly interconnected and required someone who could understand both the technical and operational aspects of the organization.

# Technical Challenges

During my first three months, the biggest challenges included:

* Learning Coda and understanding its database structure
* Understanding the company's business processes
* Managing interconnected operational databases

Although the learning curve was significant, my previous database administration experience helped me understand data relationships and system organization more quickly.
