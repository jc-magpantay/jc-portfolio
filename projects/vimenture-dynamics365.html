Microsoft Dynamics 365 Sales CRM Administration & Lead Operations

Implemented Dynamics 365 Sales to centralize customer data, improve lead imports, and strengthen sales operations for Vimenture LLC.

CRM Administration Vimenture LLC • PropTech 2024 – 2026

Project Overview

Vimenture LLC is an MSP and technology consulting company specializing in managed telecommunications and IT solutions for multifamily housing and senior living communities. I worked as the CRM Administrator / Database Administrator to implement Microsoft Dynamics 365 Sales as the organization's centralized CRM platform and single source of truth.

Background & Business Problem

Sales and marketing relied on multiple disconnected systems (Audex360, Apollo.io, Excel, Google Sheets, and legacy customer records), causing duplicate records, manual lead imports, inconsistent information, poor pipeline visibility, and time-consuming lead management.

  • Challenge 1: Duplicate customer records and inconsistent data
  • Challenge 2: Manual lead imports and poor pipeline visibility
  • Challenge 3: Alignment between Sales and Marketing

My Role

I served as the CRM Administrator and Database Administrator. I independently learned Dynamics 365, designed CRM data structures and custom fields, configured views and pipelines, imported and cleaned lead data, eliminated duplicates, built Power Automate flows, managed users, and provided reporting and support to Sales and Marketing.

  • Administered Microsoft Dynamics 365 Sales and Dataverse
  • Designed custom fields, views, and Opportunity stages
  • Performed lead imports, data validation, and duplicate removal

Technologies & Tools Used

Platform & Tools:
  • Microsoft Dynamics 365 Sales
  • Microsoft Dataverse
  • Power Automate, Power Apps
Skills Applied:
  • CRM Administration
  • Data Migration & Validation
  • Sales Operations & Business Analysis

Challenges & Obstacles

Major challenges included learning Dynamics 365 and Dataverse, ensuring data quality for large lead imports, and aligning CRM structures with actual sales workflows while working independently without a dedicated mentor.

Challenge: Importing and cleaning thousands of leads while preventing duplicates and preserving data integrity

Solution: Performed extensive cleanup and validation prior to import, built views and processes to reduce duplicates, and created small Power Automate flows to streamline operations.

Solution & Approach

I gradually configured Dynamics 365 Sales as the central CRM: standardized records, created custom fields and Opportunity stages, imported cleaned leads, built workflow automations, and supported users during adoption.

  1. Standardized CRM data model and created custom fields mapped to business needs
  2. Cleaned and validated lead data from Apollo.io and other sources before import
  3. Configured views and pipelines to simplify sales activities
  4. Implemented small Power Automate flows to automate repetitive tasks

Results & Outcome

The project established Dynamics 365 Sales as the centralized CRM, improving lead organization, reducing duplicates, and enabling better collaboration between Sales and Marketing.

Centralized

Customer information consolidated across systems

Cleaner Data

Improved data consistency and fewer duplicates

Improved Ops

Simpler sales workflows and better pipeline visibility

Foundation

Platform ready for future automation and analytics

Key Takeaways & Lessons Learned

This project accelerated my knowledge of Microsoft Dynamics 365 and Dataverse, improved my ability to translate business requirements into CRM structures, and reinforced working independently to deliver practical, production-ready CRM solutions.

  • Lesson 1: Invest in data cleanup before imports to prevent downstream issues
  • Lesson 2: Small automations can significantly reduce repetitive work
  • Lesson 3: Align CRM structures with actual user workflows to improve adoption
